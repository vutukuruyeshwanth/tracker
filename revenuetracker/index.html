Empowering Araku Through Community Tourism

Building sustainable tourism that benefits local communities, preserves culture, and creates lasting economic opportunities in the beautiful Araku Valley.

Our Approach

🌱

Sustainable Development

Eco-friendly tourism practices that preserve Araku's natural beauty while generating income.

🤝

Community Ownership

📊

Transparent Tracking

Real-time revenue monitoring ensures equitable distribution across all stakeholders.

Community-Based Tourism Model

🏡 Homestays

Local families open their homes to visitors, providing authentic cultural experiences.

Revenue Share: 60% Family, 40% Community Fund

🥾 Guided Tours

Community members lead nature walks, cultural tours, and adventure activities.

Revenue Share: 70% Guide, 30% Community Fund

🍽️ Local Cuisine

Traditional food experiences showcasing Araku's unique tribal cuisine and coffee culture.

Revenue Share: 65% Cook, 35% Community Fund

🎨 Handicrafts

Local artisans sell traditional crafts, bamboo products, and tribal artwork.

Revenue Share: 80% Artisan, 20% Community Fund
